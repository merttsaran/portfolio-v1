About Me

With over 8 years of experience working at Hapag‑Lloyd, a major global shipping company, I built deep expertise in liner operations, customs, supply chain processes, and international logistics. My time in this senior role taught me how global systems work, how to identify bottlenecks, and how to drive improvements that matter in real‑world operations.

Over time, I realized my favorite part of my work was solving complex problems — and I began exploring software development as the best medium to build solutions that are scalable, automated, and future‑ready. That's why I’m transitioning into Full‑Stack Development to combine practical operational insight with modern web engineering.

I’ve built a strong foundation in HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, and I’m actively building projects to showcase my ability to turn ideas into working interfaces. My goal is to create intuitive, efficient digital tools that help teams work smarter — especially in logistics and supply‑chain focused domains.
